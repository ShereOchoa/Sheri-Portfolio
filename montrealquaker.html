Reimagining the MQM newsletter

A modern approach to a traditional format

An organization with a heart of gold, doing amazing work to make the world a better place was looking to modernize their newsletter publishing process. They had been using Microsoft Word to build their newsletter and manually sending it to their members.

The process

After diving into the organization's needs, I conducted market research to identify the best tools and software to use for creating the newsletter. I assessed various options and suggested a cloud-based email marketing platform with easy-to-use templates and analytics.

Together with the organization, I developed a modern and attractive design. I applied my web development skills to integrated the new design with the email marketing platform. This allowed having a user-friendly publishing process that didn't require technical expertise from the editors.

I provided training to the organization's members on how to use the new tools, making sure they were comfortable taking over the process and maintaining it for the long-term.

The results were immediate, users praised the more engaging and informative design!
