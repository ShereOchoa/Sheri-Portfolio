Hi there, I'm Sheri Ochoa!

I'm a UX/UI researcher and front-end developer based in Montreal.
I create digital products that meet the needs of users and deliver value to the organization. My ultimate goal is to create intuitive, functional, and visually appealing user interfaces that work across all devices.

A little bit more about me...

As a UX designer with a multidisciplinary background, I have a strong foundation in both human psychology and research. Leveraging my expertise, I conduct thorough user research and design experiences that cater to diverse populations. I prioritize user-centered design, constantly seeking to bring the user's perspective into the design life cycle. I believe that the user is the ultimate expert in their own experience, and strive to create interfaces that meet their needs and expectations.
In addition to my design work, I enjoy exploring various creative pursuits such as fabricating artifacts, experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen, and spending quality time with my furry friend.
